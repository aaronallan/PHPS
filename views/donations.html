For 150 years, Palapala Ho'omau Church has provided a sanctuary where residents and visitors may be in solitary peace or gather in grace.

To continue its role in the life of the Kipahulu community and beyond as a place of worship, burials, celebrations, music, and meetings, Palapala Ho'omau requires preservation and maintenance. Donations are accepted to build the endowment to maintain the grounds and graves.
